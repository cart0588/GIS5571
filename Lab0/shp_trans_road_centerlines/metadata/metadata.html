Originator Aitkin County GIS
Title Road Centerlines, Aitkin County, Minnesota

Purpose To have an E-911 road spatial database so that street addresses could be assigned to residences in accordance with E-911.

Attribute Accuracy No complete accuracy checks have been made. Errors have been known to occur during data entry and have been corrected as they are found. All Mandatory fields per NG911 requirements are populated as are Conditional fields where a value exists.
